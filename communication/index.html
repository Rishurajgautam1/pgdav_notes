ENGLISH COMMUNICATION

COMMUNICATION :- The Communication is a two-way process where the message in the form of ideas, thoughts, feelings, opinions is transmitted between two or more persons with the intent of creating a shared understanding.

PROCESS OF COMMUNICATION

1. SENDER
2. ENCODER
3. MESSAGE
4. CHANNEL
5. RECEIVER
6. DECODER

FUNCTIONS OF BUSSINESS COMMUNICATION

1. Inform Employees About Job Functions
2. Persuade Clients and Partners
3. Motivate Employees to Make Better Decisions
4. Socialize to Create Bonds
